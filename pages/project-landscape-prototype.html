⚠︎ Landscape ⚠︎: Prototype

Video Prototipo

Landscape was created as part of the Expressive Media 3 subject, where I developed an interactive prototype in Figma to show a series of abstract landscapes from a previous work. The project focuses on the exploration of different digital media, combining images, textures and altered photos to create unique landscapes that challenge traditional perceptions of the environment.


The prototype in Figma organizes and presents in a clear and visual way the different categories of elements used in the creative process. Through the interactive interface, the user can explore how raster textures, edited images, and digital manipulation effects are integrated into the construction of abstract landscapes. Each section is divided by type of asset used (raster textures, edited images, etc.), offering a detailed look at the composition and design process.


This project seeks not only to show the final result, but also to invite the viewer to discover the process behind each creation and how the interaction of the different visual elements gives rise to abstract compositions.
